# 80/20 (Dominant 50/50)

**Category:** Leg Entanglement

## How to Identify

Looks like 50/50 but ONE player has their OUTSIDE LEG crossing OVER the opponent's hip/body, giving them dominant control. The dominant player's legs are higher on the opponent. Asymmetric — one player has significantly more control than the other.
