# 80/20 (Dominant 50/50)

**Category:** Leg Entanglement

## How to Identify

Starts as 50/50 but ONE player has broken the symmetry — their OUTSIDE foot/leg is crossing OVER the opponent's HIP or body, with their knee-line pushed higher onto the opponent's torso. **Disambiguator vs `fifty_fifty`:** asymmetric — the dominant player's outside foot is ACROSS (not beside) the opponent. **vs `outside_ashi`:** both legs of both players are still engaged in a 50/50-style mirror (not a single opponent leg isolated in an ashi frame). The dominant player's heel is typically hidden (safe); the controlled player's heel is often exposed — heel-hook threat for the dominant side.
