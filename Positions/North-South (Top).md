# North-South (Top)

**Category:** Dominant Position (Top)

## How to Identify

Top person is lying ON TOP but HEAD-TO-TOE (180 degrees). Both people's heads point in OPPOSITE directions. Top person's chest is on the bottom person's chest but they are facing opposite ways.
