# Wrestling Scramble

**Category:** Scramble / Transition

## How to Identify

Fast movement, bodies tangled, neither person in a clear position. Someone may be shooting for a takedown, sprawling, or scrambling to their feet. Very dynamic and hard to freeze-frame.
