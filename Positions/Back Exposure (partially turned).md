# Back Exposure (partially turned)

**Category:** Scramble / Transition

## How to Identify

One person is partially turned away showing their BACK to the other but not fully in turtle or back control yet. Mid-transition — could go to back take or guard recovery. The exposed person's shoulders are rotating away.
