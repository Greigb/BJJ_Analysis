# Cross Ashi Garami

**Category:** Leg Entanglement

## How to Identify

Attacker controls ONE of the opponent's legs (isolated between the attacker's own legs, ashi-family) and has angled their OWN body ACROSS — torso and hips rotated roughly perpendicular to the opponent's hips, so the attacker's legs cross to the opposite side of the opponent's body. **Disambiguator vs `straight_ashi`:** attacker's chest is NOT parallel to the opponent's trapped leg — it is rotated ~90°. **vs `outside_ashi`:** attacker's outside foot is across the opposite hip (not the same-side hip). **vs `inside_sankaku`:** attacker's legs are NOT figure-foured around the opponent's leg — they cross the body, not each other. Transitional position often used to switch between inside and outside sankaku; heel exposure depends on which way the attacker is rotated.
