# 50/50

**Category:** Leg Entanglement

## How to Identify

Both people have their legs EQUALLY entangled — each has one leg wrapped around the other's leg, mirror-image symmetrical at the knee. Both face the SAME direction, legs intertwined between hips and knees, neither decisively on top. **Disambiguator vs `eighty_twenty`:** neither player's outside foot crosses OVER the opponent's body/hip — feet stay BESIDE the opponent's knee. **vs `backside_fifty_fifty`:** both players face FORWARD (not back-to-back). **vs `inside_sankaku`:** BOTH legs mirror-trapped, not one leg isolated between attacker's thighs. Call out whose heel is exposed past the opponent's hip — that is the threatened heel.
