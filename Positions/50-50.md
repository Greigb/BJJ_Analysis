# 50/50

**Category:** Leg Entanglement

## How to Identify

Both people have their legs EQUALLY entangled — each has one leg wrapped around the other's leg in a MIRROR image. Symmetrical position. Both people face the same direction with legs intertwined. Neither has a clear advantage.
