# Passing Open Guard (Top)

**Category:** Inside Guard (Top)

## How to Identify

Top person standing or kneeling OVER the bottom person who is playing open guard. Trying to get past the legs. Gripping pants/ankles, stepping around feet. Not yet past the legs.
