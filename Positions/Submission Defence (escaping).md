# Submission Defence (escaping)

**Category:** Scramble / Transition

## How to Identify

One person is actively defending a submission — hands are fighting at the neck (defending choke), or stacking/posturing to escape an armbar, or rolling to relieve heel hook pressure. The defensive action is the primary thing happening.
