# Closed Guard (Bottom)

**Category:** Guard (Bottom)

## How to Identify

Person on their BACK with LEGS WRAPPED around the other person's waist. Ankles are LOCKED behind the top person's back. The top person is between the bottom person's legs, chest-to-chest.
