# Single Leg X / Ashi Garami

**Category:** Leg Entanglement

## How to Identify

Bottom person is on their BACK with BOTH LEGS controlling ONE of the opponent's legs. One foot is on the hip, the other hooks behind the knee. The opponent's leg is between the attacker's legs. The opponent is usually still standing or kneeling.
