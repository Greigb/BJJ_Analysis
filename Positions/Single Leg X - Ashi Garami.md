# Single Leg X / Ashi Garami

**Category:** Leg Entanglement

## How to Identify

Bottom person is on their BACK with BOTH of their legs controlling ONE of the opponent's legs — one foot rests on the opponent's HIP, the other HOOKS BEHIND the opponent's KNEE, forming a "V" around the single trapped leg. **Disambiguator vs `straight_ashi`:** the opponent is STILL STANDING or kneeling (not seated/flat), and the attacker's foot is PINNED BEHIND the opponent's knee rather than lying alongside. **vs `inside_sankaku`:** attacker's feet are in a V (open angle), not figure-foured around the trapped leg. The opponent's heel is exposed past the attacker's hip on the same side as the foot-on-hip placement; sweeps + heel-hook entries both follow.
