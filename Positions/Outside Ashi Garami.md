# Outside Ashi Garami

**Category:** Leg Entanglement

## How to Identify

Similar to straight_ashi — opponent's ONE leg trapped between attacker's legs — but the attacker's OUTSIDE (top) foot CROSSES OVER the opponent's body or far hip, so the outside foot ends up on the FAR side of the opponent. **Disambiguator vs `straight_ashi`:** attacker's outside foot is ACROSS (not beside) the opponent's body. **vs `inside_sankaku`:** opponent's leg is NOT between the attacker's thighs — the ashi frame is beside/outside the knee, with the trapping done by the outside foot's position across the body. **vs `cross_ashi`:** attacker's chest is parallel to the opponent's leg (not rotated ~90°). Sets up outside heel hook; the trapped-leg heel is exposed on the OUTSIDE of the attacker's body.
