# Dogfight / Underhook Battle

**Category:** Scramble / Transition

## How to Identify

Both people are on their KNEES facing each other, chest-to-chest, fighting for underhooks. Neither is clearly on top or bottom. A 50/50 wrestling position. Both trying to get underneath the other.
