# Outside Sankaku (411)

**Category:** Leg Entanglement

## How to Identify

Attacker has legs triangled around the opponent's leg but from the OUTSIDE. The attacker's body faces AWAY from the opponent (belly-down relative to the opponent's leg). The triangle grip is on the outside of the thigh. Used for outside heel hooks.
