# Front Headlock / Guillotine Position

**Category:** Scramble / Transition

## How to Identify

One person has their arm wrapped around the OTHER person's HEAD/NECK from the front. The head is trapped under the armpit. Often the defender's head is down. Can be standing or on the ground. The controller's body is sprawled back.
