# Butterfly Guard

**Category:** Guard (Bottom)

## How to Identify

Bottom person is SEATED UPRIGHT (not flat on back) with BOTH FEET hooked inside the top person's thighs. Shins are against the inner thighs. Bottom person often has an underhook.
