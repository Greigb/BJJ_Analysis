# Mount (Top)

**Category:** Dominant Position (Top)

## How to Identify

Top person is SITTING ON the bottom person's torso/hips, STRADDLING them. Knees on both sides of the body. The bottom person is flat on their back underneath. Top person is upright or leaning forward.
