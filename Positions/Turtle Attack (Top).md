# Turtle Attack (Top)

**Category:** Scramble / Transition

## How to Identify

One person is on top of or beside the turtled person (who is on hands and knees). The attacker is draped over the turtle, reaching for the neck or trying to insert hooks. Often has a seatbelt grip (one arm over shoulder, one under armpit).
