# Side Control (Top)

**Category:** Dominant Position (Top)

## How to Identify

Top person is lying PERPENDICULAR (90 degrees) across the bottom person's chest. Chest-to-chest contact. NO LEGS between them — the pass is complete. Top person's hips are heavy on the mat. Often controlling with crossface and underhook.
