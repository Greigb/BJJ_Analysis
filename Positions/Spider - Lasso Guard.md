# Spider / Lasso Guard

**Category:** Guard (Bottom)

## How to Identify

Bottom person on back with FEET ON THE BICEPS of the top person, pushing the arms. Legs are extended, creating distance. Often seen with gi grips on sleeves. Lasso: one leg wraps around the arm.
