# Inside Closed Guard (Top)

**Category:** Inside Guard (Top)

## How to Identify

Person kneeling or postured INSIDE the other's CLOSED (locked ankles) guard. Trapped between the bottom person's wrapped legs. Often hands on the bottom person's hips or chest trying to open the guard.
