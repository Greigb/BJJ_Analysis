# Honey hole entry

## Used from

- [[Cross Ashi Garami]]
- [[Outside Ashi Garami]]
- [[Single Leg X / Ashi Garami]]
- [[Straight Ashi Garami]]

## How to Identify

The attacker's top leg pummels up and over the opponent's thigh while the bottom leg threads underneath, closing a figure-four around the trapped leg with the shin pinned across the top of the thigh. Hips rotate perpendicular to the opponent's hip line and the attacker falls away to the outside, committing weight off the leg so the knee line is isolated inside the saddle.
