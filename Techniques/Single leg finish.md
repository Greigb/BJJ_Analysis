# Single leg finish

## Used from

- [[Dogfight / Underhook Battle]]

## How to Identify

Both of the attacker's arms are clamped around one of the opponent's legs at the knee or thigh, head pinned tight to the hip, with the captured limb elevated against their chest. Weight drives forward through the shoulder as they post a knee up from the ground, bumping or running the pipe to topple the opponent sideways off their base.
