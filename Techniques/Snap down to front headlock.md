# Snap down to front headlock

## Used from

- [[Turtle Attack (Top)]]

## How to Identify

The attacker faces the turtled opponent's head with chest pressing down on the back of the neck, one arm threaded under the throat into a chin strap or gable grip while the other traps the near arm. The opponent's forehead is snapped toward the mat as their posture collapses forward out of turtle, the attacker's hips low and weight driving through the crown of the head.
