# Armbar

## Used from

- [[Back Mount / Back Control]]
- [[Closed Guard (Bottom)]]
- [[Crucifix]]
- [[Knee on Belly]]

## How to Identify

The attacker is on their back or side with hips perpendicular to the opponent's shoulder, both legs clamped across the torso and head, knees pinched tightly together. The opponent's arm is fully extended between the attacker's thighs, wrist pulled to the chest with the thumb pointing up, while the attacker's hips arch upward into the elbow joint.
