# Log splitter

## Used from

- [[Inside Closed Guard (Top)]]
- [[Mount (Top)]]
- [[North-South (Top)]]

## How to Identify

One of the bottom person's legs is scooped high onto the top person's shoulder with the knee driven toward the bottom person's own face, while the other leg is pinned flat or pushed aside. The top person is postured upright, hips stacking forward and down, with weight crashing through the trapped leg. The bottom person's legs are visibly split wide at the hips rather than clamped in guard, pelvis tilted and angled off to one side.
