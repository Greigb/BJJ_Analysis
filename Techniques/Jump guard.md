# Jump guard

## Used from

- [[Guard Recovery (in progress)]]

## How to Identify

One athlete is briefly airborne with both legs clamping around a standing opponent's waist, typically while gripping collar-and-sleeve or a double collar tie. The jumper's hips lift off the mat and their feet cross or hook behind the opponent's back, with the standing partner momentarily bearing the jumper's full body weight.
