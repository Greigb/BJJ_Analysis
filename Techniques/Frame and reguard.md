# Frame and reguard

## Used from

- [[Guard Recovery (in progress)]]

## How to Identify

The bottom player braces a forearm frame into the top player's hip or neck while shrimping their hips out at an angle, creating visible separation. The inside knee simultaneously threads between the two bodies, shin or knee wedging against the opponent's torso to rebuild a guard barrier as weight shifts onto the outside shoulder and hip.
