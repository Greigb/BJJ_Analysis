# Stack pass

## Used from

- [[Inside Closed Guard (Top)]]
- [[Mount (Top)]]
- [[North-South (Top)]]

## How to Identify

Top player postures tall and drives the bottom's knees up toward their face, folding the hips off the mat so the bottom player's weight loads onto their own shoulders and neck. Watch for hands gripping pants or hamstrings with elbows pinched in, a shoulder wedged into a thigh, and the passer angling toward one hip to walk around the stacked legs.
