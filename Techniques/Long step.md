# Long step

## Used from

- [[Passing Open Guard (Top)]]

## How to Identify

Top passer stands or kneels over open guard with grips on the bottom player's pants or ankles pinning one leg down, then swings the far leg in a deep arcing step back and around the controlled leg. The hip briefly rotates away from the opponent as weight loads onto the posting leg, and the stepping foot lands past the bottom player's hip line to clear the legs.
