# Balloon sweep

## Used from

- [[Spider / Lasso Guard]]

## How to Identify

Bottom player is supine with legs fully extended, feet planted on the top player's hips or thighs, elevating them skyward. Sleeve or wrist grips pull down toward the bottom player's chest as the top player's weight tips forward and their knees lift off the mat, leaving them briefly suspended overhead.
