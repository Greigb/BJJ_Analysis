# Posture break

## Used from

- [[Submission Defence (escaping)]]

## How to Identify

The attacker's grips load onto the collar, lapel, or back of the neck and yank downward as the defender tries to posture up or stack free of a submission; the defender's head drops below shoulder line and the spine curves into a C-shape. Elbows draw tight to the chest and legs climb high on the back or hips to amplify the pull, flattening the defender's base forward.
