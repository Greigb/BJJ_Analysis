# Collar drag

## Used from

- [[Open Guard (generic)]]
- [[Scramble (generic)]]

## How to Identify

The bottom player has a deep cross-collar grip and is actively pulling the opponent's head and shoulders down and across their centerline. The opponent's posture breaks sharply forward, head dragged past the bottom player's near hip and exposing the shoulder, as the bottom player rotates onto their side toward the drag. A sleeve or wrist grip on the opposite arm often kills the post on that side.
