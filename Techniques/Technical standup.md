# Technical standup

## Used from

- [[Sweep in Progress]]

## How to Identify

One hand posts flat on the mat behind the hips while the opposite foot stays planted with knee bent, forming a tripod base. The hips lift clear of the ground as the same-side leg swings through underneath, and the free hand frames toward the opponent while the torso rotates over the posted shoulder.
