# Backstep to leg lock

## Used from

- [[Headquarters / Passing Position]]

## How to Identify

Top player pivots their hips away from the pass, swinging the outside leg back and dropping into a low squat with their back rotating toward the bottom player's head. Weight transfers from forward pressure into a seated position, hands often posting on the hip or thigh as one of the bottom player's legs is trapped between the knees.
