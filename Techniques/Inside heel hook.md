# Inside heel hook

## Used from

- [[Cross Ashi Garami]]
- [[Inside Sankaku (Honeyhole/Saddle)]]
- [[Single Leg X / Ashi Garami]]
- [[Ushiro Ashi Garami (Rear Ashi)]]

## How to Identify

The opponent's foot sits across the attacker's chest with the heel rotated INWARD toward the attacker's far shoulder and the toes pointing across the centerline; one forearm wedges under the Achilles, hands locked in a figure-four or gable grip, while the hips rotate AWAY from the captured knee to torque the heel medially. Finishes from `inside_sankaku` / saddle / honey hole — opponent's leg is BETWEEN the attacker's thighs. **Disambiguator vs `outside_heel_hook`:** twist direction is INWARD (toward the opponent's midline); the trapped knee rotates TOWARD the centerline. **vs `toe_hold`:** grip is around the HEEL/Achilles, not the toes — watch for the forearm blade under the Achilles, not cupping the top of the foot.
