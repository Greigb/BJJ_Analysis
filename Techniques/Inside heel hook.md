# Inside heel hook

## Used from

- [[Cross Ashi Garami]]
- [[Inside Sankaku (Honeyhole/Saddle)]]
- [[Single Leg X / Ashi Garami]]
- [[Ushiro Ashi Garami (Rear Ashi)]]

## How to Identify

The opponent's foot sits across the attacker's chest with the heel rotated inward toward the attacker's far shoulder and the toes pointing across the centerline; one forearm wedges under the Achilles, hands locked in a figure-four or gable grip, while the hips rotate away from the captured knee to torque the heel medially.
