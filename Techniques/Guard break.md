# Guard break

## Used from

- [[Inside Closed Guard (Top)]]
- [[Mount (Top)]]
- [[North-South (Top)]]

## How to Identify

Top player postures upright with spine straight and head up, hands planted firmly on the bottom player's hips or sternum and elbows clamped tight against their own knees. One knee drives into the tailbone or wedges between the thighs while hips press forward, prying the crossed ankles apart as the bottom player's legs are forced to unhook.
