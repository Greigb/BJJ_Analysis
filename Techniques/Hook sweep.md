# Hook sweep

## Used from

- [[Butterfly Guard]]
- [[Sweep in Progress]]

## How to Identify

The bottom player falls away from their underhook side while the butterfly hook on that same side visibly elevates the top player's thigh off the mat, lifting one leg clear of the ground. The top player's base breaks as their weight tips sideways over the bottom player's shoulder, rotating through the reversal with hips airborne rather than planted.
