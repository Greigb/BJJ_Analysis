# Transition to backside 50/50

## Used from

- [[50/50]]
- [[80/20 (Dominant 50/50)]]
- [[Inside Sankaku (Honeyhole/Saddle)]]

## How to Identify

The attacker is mid-spin, rotating to face away from the opponent while legs stay entangled so the pair ends hip-to-hip but head-to-foot. The outside leg swings across the opponent's far hip and the attacker turns belly down or onto the outside shoulder, exposing the back of the trapped heel.
