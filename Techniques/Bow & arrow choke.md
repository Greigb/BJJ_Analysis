# Bow & arrow choke

## Used from

- [[Back Mount / Back Control]]

## How to Identify

The attacker is rotated off to one side of the opponent's back with a deep cross-collar grip sunk behind the neck, while the other hand pulls the far arm across like drawing a bowstring. One leg swings over the trapped shoulder as the attacker falls back, wrenching the opponent's head down toward the attacker's hip.
