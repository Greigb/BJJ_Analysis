# Scissor sweep

## Used from

- [[Sweep in Progress]]

## How to Identify

The bottom player rotates onto their side with one shin angled across the top player's belly and the other leg chopping low behind the knees, scissoring together as the sweep fires. A cross-sleeve plus collar or lapel grip pulls the top player forward and across, tipping them over the chopping-leg side as their base collapses.
