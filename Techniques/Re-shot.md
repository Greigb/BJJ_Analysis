# Re-shot

## Used from

- [[Wrestling Scramble]]

## How to Identify

A Re-shot appears when the attacker's initial takedown attempt is stuffed or sprawled on, and rather than disengaging, they immediately chain into a second shot on the same or opposite leg. Look for a level change back down after being flattened, hips dropping again with the lead knee sliding toward the mat, and hands reaching past the defender's sprawl to re-grip the leg or ankle while the defender is still recovering their base.
