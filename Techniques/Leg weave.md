# Leg weave

## Used from

- [[Headquarters / Passing Position]]

## How to Identify

The top player threads one arm under the bottom player's top leg and grips the far collar, lapel or shoulder, folding the trapped leg across the bottom player's torso. Chest and shoulder pressure drive down onto the weaved thigh while the passer's head stays on the near side, hips low and angled to kill frames.
