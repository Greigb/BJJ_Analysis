# Arm triangle

## Used from

- [[Side Control (Top)]]

## How to Identify

The top player's shoulder presses into the side of the bottom player's neck, trapping the bottom player's near arm across their own throat. Hands are clasped in a gable or palm-to-palm grip behind the head with the top player's own head tight to their bicep, while the hips often slide off to the far side as weight pours forward through the shoulder.
