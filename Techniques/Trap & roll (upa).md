# Trap & roll (upa)

## Used from

- [[Bottom Mount]]

## How to Identify

Bottom player is flat on their back under mount with one of the opponent's arms clamped tight across their chest and the same-side foot hooked to trap the ankle. Hips bridge explosively upward and the shoulders rotate toward the trapped side as the opponent, unable to post, tips over.
