# Turn to guard

## Used from

- [[Back Taken (Defending)]]

## How to Identify

The defender drops a shoulder to the mat and corkscrews their hips toward the attacker, turning their head to face them. A knee swings across to clear the top hook or break the body triangle while a frame on the biceps or shoulder drives chest-to-chest, closing the back exposure.
