# Knee slide

## Used from

- [[Headquarters / Passing Position]]
- [[Pass in Progress]]
- [[Passing Open Guard (Top)]]

## How to Identify

The top player's lead knee cuts diagonally across the bottom person's near thigh, shin pinning it down, while the trailing leg posts wide for base. An underhook or cross-collar grip traps the near shoulder as weight drives forward over the sliding knee and the hip lowers toward the mat.
