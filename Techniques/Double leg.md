# Double leg

## Used from

- [[Standing - Neutral]]

## How to Identify

Attacker drops level with hips below the opponent's, head pressed tight to one side of the hip, and both arms wrap the back of both legs at knee height with hands clasped. The lead leg penetrates deep between the opponent's feet as the shoulder drives forward into the midsection, loading the opponent's weight rearward onto their heels.
