# Mount transition

## Used from

- [[Knee on Belly]]
- [[Side Control (Top)]]

## How to Identify

A leg is mid-travel across the bottom player's torso or hips while the chest stays pinned to block half-guard recovery. The top player's hips rotate from perpendicular toward parallel with the body, weight driving down through the sternum as the knee clears. A hand typically posts on the mat or cups the head and far shoulder to stop the shrimp before the leg lands.
