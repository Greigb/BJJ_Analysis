# Imanari roll entry

## Used from

- [[Guard Recovery (in progress)]]

## How to Identify

A forward shoulder roll dives under the opponent's lead leg with the head tucked and the trailing leg whipping overhead, the body spiraling low across the mat. The hips invert toward the opponent's knee line and the legs snap shut into an ashi-garami wrap, leaving the attacker on their back already entangled on the target leg.
