# Boot scoot

## Used from

- [[Back Taken (Defending)]]

## How to Identify

Defender is flat on their back with the attacker chest-to-back behind them, hooks or body triangle in, while planting both heels and walking the feet to slide the hips downward toward the mat. Hands fight at the collar or wrists defending the choke as the attacker's hooks visibly stretch out or the body triangle lengthens with the scoot.
