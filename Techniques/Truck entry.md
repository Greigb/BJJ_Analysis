# Truck entry

## Used from

- [[Back Exposure (partially turned)]]
- [[Turtle Attack (Top)]]

## How to Identify

The attacker drops their shoulders below the turtled opponent's hip line and threads a leg through to figure-four around one of the opponent's thighs, isolating a single leg. Watch for the attacker rotating underneath as their legs triangle a trapped thigh, often peeling the opponent's near knee off the mat as the entanglement closes.
