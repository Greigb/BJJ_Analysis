# Elbow-knee escape

## Used from

- [[Bottom Mount]]

## How to Identify

Bottom player is turned onto one side with hips shrimped out and shoulder blades off the mat, head and chest angled away from the mount. A forearm frames into the opponent's near hip or thigh while the same-side knee wedges up through the gap between that frame and the opponent's knee, sliding across toward the centerline.
