# Kimura counter

## Used from

- [[North-South (Bottom)]]

## How to Identify

The bottom player traps the attacker's near-side wrist against their own hip or ribs, then shoots their opposite arm across to grip their own wrist, forming a figure-four on the attacker's arm from underneath. Their elbows are tight to their torso and their shoulders rotate toward the attacker to load the kimura grip, often bridging into the attacker's chest to create space and break the smother. The defender's head usually turns away from the trapped arm as they start to come up onto a hip.
