# Underhook escape

## Used from

- [[Bottom Side Control]]

## How to Identify

The bottom player is turned onto their side rather than flat, with their near-side arm threaded deep beneath the top player's far armpit to establish an underhook, shoulder driving up into the armpit. Their hips have shrimped out to create an angle, often with the free hand framing on the opponent's hip or biceps, and you can see the top player's chest being elevated and rotated as the bottom player comes up onto their elbow or shoulder to recover guard or take the back.
