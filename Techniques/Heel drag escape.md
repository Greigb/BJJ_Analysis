# Heel drag escape

## Used from

- [[Bottom Mount]]

## How to Identify

Bottom player lies flat under mount, one leg extended with the heel hooked over the opponent's instep and dragging that foot back toward their own hip. The opponent's base buckles on the trapped side as the bottom player bridges and rotates toward the pinned foot, loading weight into the shoulder opposite the drag.
