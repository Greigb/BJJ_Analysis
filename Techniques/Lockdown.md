# Lockdown

## Used from

- [[Half Guard (Bottom)]]

## How to Identify

The bottom player is on their side in half guard with their legs figure-foured around the top player's trapped leg, bottom leg hooking under and top leg crossing over to catch the instep behind the knee. Hips are extended forward to stretch and immobilise the trapped leg, often paired with a same-side underhook and the head off the mat.
