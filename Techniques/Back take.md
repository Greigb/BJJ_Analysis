# Back take

## Used from

- [[Backside 50/50]]
- [[De La Riva Guard]]
- [[Dogfight / Underhook Battle]]

## How to Identify

One player climbs chest-to-back behind the other as a seatbelt grip forms — one arm over the shoulder, the other under the far armpit, hands meeting on the chest. Hooks are being threaded inside the thighs from behind, and the attacker's hips ride high on the defender's lower back with the shoulder blades rotating toward the mat.
