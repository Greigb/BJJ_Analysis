# Triangle setup

## Used from

- [[Spider / Lasso Guard]]

## How to Identify

Bottom player is on their back with at least one foot planted on the top player's bicep while the other leg climbs high onto the shoulder or begins shooting across the neck, often after a sleeve grip pulls one arm deep across the centerline. Hips are angled off-center rather than square, and the top player is posted on one hand with weight stacked forward as the bottom player's shin threatens to cut across the throat.
