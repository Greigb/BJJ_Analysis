# Inside trip

## Used from

- [[Clinch / Collar Tie]]
- [[Unclear / Cannot Determine]]

## How to Identify

From a clinch or collar tie, the attacker's lead leg steps deep between the opponent's thighs and hooks behind the inside of the far knee or calf. Torsos stay chest-to-chest with an underhook or strong collar grip, while the hips drive forward and slightly across, tipping the opponent's weight backward over the trapped, posted leg.
