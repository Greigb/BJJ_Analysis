# Sweep to top 50/50

## Used from

- [[50/50]]
- [[80/20 (Dominant 50/50)]]

## How to Identify

Legs stay fully mirrored and entangled throughout the motion while one player drives their shoulder toward the mat and shifts their hips under, tipping the other sideways with the inside hook. The rising player typically grips the opponent's near ankle or pant cuff and posts on an elbow or hand to come up, with neither pair of legs ever disengaging from the entanglement.
