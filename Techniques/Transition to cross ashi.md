# Transition to cross ashi

## Used from

- [[Outside Sankaku (411)]]

## How to Identify

The outside triangle unhooks and the shins reconfigure into an X across the opponent's trapped leg, one shin pressing over the thigh while the other ankle crosses to lock it. The hips rotate from belly-down toward neutral during the switch, and knee-line control stays preserved so the leg cannot escape mid-reconfiguration.
