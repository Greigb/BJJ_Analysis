# Inversion

## Used from

- [[Guard Recovery (in progress)]]

## How to Identify

The bottom player is rotated onto their upper back and shoulders with hips lifted higher than the head, often with one shoulder driving into the mat and the spine curled. Legs are swinging up and overhead or sweeping across to one side rather than framing in front, and a hand or elbow typically posts on the mat to control the rotation. Their hips are clearly above shoulder line and the head is tucked under, momentarily upside-down beneath the top player's base.
