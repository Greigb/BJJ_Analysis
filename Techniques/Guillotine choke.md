# Guillotine choke

## Used from

- [[Front Headlock / Guillotine Position]]

## How to Identify

One arm encircles the opponent's neck from the front with the head pinched tight under the armpit and both hands clasped beneath the chin, often palm-to-palm or gripping the choking wrist. The defender's posture is broken forward with head bowed, while the controller sprawls back or — if on the ground — closes guard with hips lifted to drive weight into the throat.
