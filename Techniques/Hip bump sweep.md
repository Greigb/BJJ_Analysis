# Hip bump sweep

## Used from

- [[Closed Guard (Bottom)]]

## How to Identify

The bottom player breaks from closed guard by posting one hand behind them on the mat, gripping the opposite-side sleeve or wrist of the top player, and exploding their hips up and forward into the top player's chest. The signature cue is a sharp diagonal hip bump combined with a sleeve-trap that pins the top player's arm, rolling them over that trapped shoulder while the bottom player comes up to mount.
