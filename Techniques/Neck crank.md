# Neck crank

## Used from

- [[Crucifix]]

## How to Identify

The attacker's forearm or wrist is wedged across the jaw or base of the skull, levering the head at an unnatural angle to the trapped torso while the bottom person's arms remain splayed and defenceless. Bodyweight drives down over the isolated skull, forcing chin toward shoulder or chest with no framing available to relieve the torque.
