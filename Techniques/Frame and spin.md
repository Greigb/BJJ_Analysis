# Frame and spin

## Used from

- [[North-South (Bottom)]]

## How to Identify

The bottom player's forearms drive up into the opponent's hips or near-side shoulder, creating a rigid frame that opens space between the chests while the head turns away from the pressure. Simultaneously their hips shrimp and pivot perpendicular to the opponent's body, legs swinging in an arc to realign, converting the smother into an angle where the opponent's weight slides past rather than down.
