# Transition to outside sankaku

## Used from

- [[Outside Ashi Garami]]

## How to Identify

The attacker's top leg swings over and hooks behind their own bottom knee, locking a figure-four triangle around the opponent's thigh. Hips rotate sharply away, often turning belly-down or onto the outside hip, with the opponent's isolated leg pinched high between the attacker's thighs and the knee line crossing the attacker's centerline.
