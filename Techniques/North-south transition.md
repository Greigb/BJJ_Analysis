# North-south transition

## Used from

- [[Side Control (Top)]]

## How to Identify

Top player pivots from perpendicular side control, walking the hips in a wide arc toward the opponent's head while chest pressure slides up past the shoulders. Hands typically cup both armpits or underhook tightly as the legs sprawl and the torso rotates, briefly rising before settling above the crown of the head.
