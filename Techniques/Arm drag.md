# Arm drag

## Used from

- [[Standing - Neutral]]

## How to Identify

The attacker secures a same-side grip on the opponent's wrist and triceps, then yanks the arm sharply across their own centerline while pivoting off-line. Watch for the opponent's shoulder and head being pulled forward past their lead foot, breaking their posture and exposing the back. The attacker's hips rotate toward the dragged arm as they step around to the opponent's flank.
