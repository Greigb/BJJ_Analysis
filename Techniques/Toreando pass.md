# Toreando pass

## Used from

- [[Passing Open Guard (Top)]]

## How to Identify

The top player stands upright with hips back, double-gripping the bottom player's pants at the shins or ankles. They shove both knees laterally while their feet step wide toward the opposite hip. The bottom player's legs stay pinned together and angled away from the direction of travel, like a bullfighter sweeping a cape.
