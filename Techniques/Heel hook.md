# Heel hook

## Used from

- [[50/50]]
- [[80/20 (Dominant 50/50)]]
- [[Backside 50/50]]

## How to Identify

The attacker traps the opponent's foot in their armpit with arms figure-foured around the lower leg, gripping across the heel. Their body rotates AWAY from the isolated leg while hips extend to torque the knee. An ashi-garami entanglement (50/50, 80/20, straight/outside/cross ashi, inside sankaku) secures the leg below, with the heel visibly exposed and the opponent's knee-line BENT off-center. **Disambiguator vs `straight_ankle_lock`:** attacker's grip is AROUND the heel itself (rotational torque), NOT cupping the Achilles under the forearm blade (extension only). **vs `kneebar`:** the opponent's knee is VISIBLY ROTATED off-center — a straight knee means no heel-hook pressure. Call out inside vs outside heel hook based on which direction the heel is being twisted.
