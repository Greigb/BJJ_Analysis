# Heel hook

## Used from

- [[50/50]]
- [[80/20 (Dominant 50/50)]]
- [[Backside 50/50]]

## How to Identify

The attacker traps the opponent's foot in their armpit with arms figure-foured around the lower leg, gripping across the heel. Their body rotates away from the isolated leg while hips extend to torque the knee. An ashi garami entanglement secures the leg, with the heel visibly exposed and the opponent's knee-line bent off-center.
