# Harness control

## Used from

- [[Back Exposure (partially turned)]]

## How to Identify

One of the attacker's arms drapes over the defender's shoulder while the other threads under the opposite armpit, hands clasped at the sternum to form a diagonal seatbelt across the torso. The attacker's chest is glued to the defender's back and their head is pressed tight on the over-shoulder side, weight driving forward through the upper back.
