# Knee shield

## Used from

- [[Half Guard (Bottom)]]

## How to Identify

The bottom player lies on their side with one shin wedged horizontally across the top player's torso or hip, knee pointing toward the opponent's far shoulder while the foot frames across the centerline. The trapped leg remains hooked around one of the opponent's legs maintaining half guard, and the bottom player's hands typically frame the biceps, collar, or sleeve to manage posture and distance. Hips are angled away from the opponent, creating space and preventing chest-to-chest connection.
