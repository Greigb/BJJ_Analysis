# DLR sweep

## Used from

- [[De La Riva Guard]]

## How to Identify

Bottom player has a De La Riva hook wrapped behind the opponent's lead knee, other foot posted on the hip, gripping the far ankle or pant cuff. Mid-sweep they extend the hip leg and pull the trapped ankle across their body, rotating onto the DLR-side shoulder to topple the standing opponent backward or to the corner.
