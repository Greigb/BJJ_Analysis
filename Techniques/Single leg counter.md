# Single leg counter

## Used from

- [[Turtle (Bottom)]]

## How to Identify

From turtle, the bottom player wraps both arms around one of the top player's legs and clasps hands at the knee, head pressed to the outside hip. Their shoulder drives into the thigh as they post off the mat and lift, running the angle to uproot the captured leg and compromise the opponent's base.
