# Seatbelt → back take

## Used from

- [[Back Exposure (partially turned)]]
- [[Turtle Attack (Top)]]

## How to Identify

Look for a seatbelt grip — one arm draped over the near shoulder, the other threaded under the far armpit, hands clasped across the chest — with the attacker's chest pressed tight to the opponent's upper back and head posted alongside. Weight drives forward and down through the shoulder blades while the legs work to insert hooks inside the hips or thighs, keeping the back exposed and the opponent unable to square up.
