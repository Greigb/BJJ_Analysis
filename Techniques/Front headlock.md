# Front headlock

## Used from

- [[Dogfight / Underhook Battle]]
- [[Wrestling Scramble]]

## How to Identify

One person's head is trapped under the other's armpit with posture broken, back rounded and head driven below their own hips. The top player sprawls chest-heavy onto the nape of the neck, one arm wrapped around the far side of the head while the other hand grips the far wrist or tricep. Body weight is stacked forward over the trapped head, flattening it down.
