# Hand fight

## Used from

- [[Back Taken (Defending)]]
- [[Submission Defence (escaping)]]

## How to Identify

The defender's hands are clamped onto the attacker's choking wrist, typically in a two-on-one grip peeling fingers away from the collar or neck. Chin is pinned tight to the chest, elbows are glued to the ribs, and shoulders are shrugged high to shield the throat from any incoming strangle.
