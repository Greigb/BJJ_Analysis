# Knee slide pass

## Used from

- [[Half Guard (Top)]]

## How to Identify

The passer's shin is angled diagonally across the trapped-side thigh of the bottom player, knee sliding toward the mat near the hip while the rear leg posts wide for base. Upper body is heavy with a crossface and far-side underhook or shoulder pressure pinning the head, hips low and driving forward at roughly 45 degrees. The bottom knee is being flattened outward as the passer's weight shears across the thigh line rather than stepping around.
