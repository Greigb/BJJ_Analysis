# Rear naked choke

## Used from

- [[Back Mount / Back Control]]

## How to Identify

Attacker is behind in back control with hooks in or a body triangle, one arm threaded under the chin so biceps and forearm form a V around the neck, the choking hand gripped into the opposite biceps while the free hand presses the back of the defender's head forward. Defender's hands typically claw at the forearm trying to peel it off.
