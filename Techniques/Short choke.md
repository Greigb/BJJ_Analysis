# Short choke

## Used from

- [[Back Mount / Back Control]]

## How to Identify

From back control with hooks or a body triangle locked in, one arm drapes over the top shoulder with the hand buried deep in the collar, thumb-in, elbow cutting down across the centerline. The bladed forearm wedges against the far-side carotid while the free hand pulls the opposite lapel taut, chest sealed tight to the upper back with no daylight.
