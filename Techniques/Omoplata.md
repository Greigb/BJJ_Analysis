# Omoplata

## Used from

- [[Closed Guard (Bottom)]]
- [[Spider / Lasso Guard]]

## How to Identify

One leg drapes over the opponent's shoulder with the shin across their upper back, trapping the near arm with the shoulder internally rotated and hand pinned low. The attacker is rotated roughly perpendicular to the opponent, hip down on the mat, typically gripping the opponent's belt or far hip to block the forward roll as the opponent's head and torso fold forward.
