# Underhook sweep

## Used from

- [[Half Guard (Bottom)]]

## How to Identify

Bottom player has secured a deep underhook on the top player's near-side arm, driving their shoulder into the opponent's armpit while coming up onto their side or elbow. The trapped leg remains locked in half guard as the bottom player's hips shift perpendicular to the opponent, head pressure angled toward the far hip. The top player's weight is visibly being lifted and rotated over the underhooked shoulder, with their base compromised toward the far side.
