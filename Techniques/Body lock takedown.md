# Body lock takedown

## Used from

- [[Clinch / Collar Tie]]
- [[Unclear / Cannot Determine]]

## How to Identify

Both arms wrap around the opponent's lower back with hands clasped in a gable or S-grip, chest sealed to chest and hips closed tight against theirs. The attacker's head is pinned to one side of the opponent's torso, and one foot typically steps deep behind or outside the opponent's lead leg as they drive forward or rotate laterally to break the base.
