# Ghost escape

## Used from

- [[Bottom Side Control]]

## How to Identify

Bottom player is shrimping hard away from the opponent's chest, hips shooting backward and angling so their head tracks toward the opponent's near hip. Forearm frames are wedged into the opponent's neck and far hip to create separation, and the bottom shoulder is peeling off the mat as the head slips out from under the chest pin toward the back door.
