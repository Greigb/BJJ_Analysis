# Lasso transition

## Used from

- [[Spider / Lasso Guard]]

## How to Identify

Bottom player's leg is threading through the opponent's same-side armpit with the shin wrapping across the upper arm, anchoring a sleeve grip on that wrist. Hips rotate off the mat to the opposite side as the lassoed arm bends and the opponent's posture collapses forward over the lasso.
