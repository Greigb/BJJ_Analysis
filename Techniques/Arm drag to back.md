# Arm drag to back

## Used from

- [[Butterfly Guard]]

## How to Identify

Bottom player has a two-on-one on the opponent's arm — cross-wrist grip with the other hand clamped high on the tricep — yanking it across their centerline. Their head drops to the outside of the dragged arm and their hips angle sharply off that same side, often elevating on the far butterfly hook as the opponent's shoulder and back rotate into view.
