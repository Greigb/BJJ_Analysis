# Shrimp to guard

## Used from

- [[Guard Recovery (in progress)]]

## How to Identify

Bottom player is turned onto their side, hips pumped away from the opponent, framing with forearms against the opponent's hip or neck while the inside knee wedges between their bodies. Space opens at the hipline as the bottom leg threads back through to rebuild a knee shield or full guard, with visible hip-escape motion rather than a static bridge.
