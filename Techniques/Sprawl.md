# Sprawl

## Used from

- [[Wrestling Scramble]]

## How to Identify

The defender's hips drop and shoot back hard while the legs kick straight out behind them, often flattening toward the mat as their chest and weight crash down onto the shooter's head, neck, and upper back. Look for a whizzer or cross-face pinning the attacker's head low, the shooter's level-change stalled with their penetration leg trapped, and the defender's belly angling toward the floor as they sprawl their weight forward over the shoulders.
