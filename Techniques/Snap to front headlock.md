# Snap to front headlock

## Used from

- [[Front Headlock / Guillotine Position]]

## How to Identify

Both hands clamp behind the opponent's head or neck and yank it sharply downward, breaking posture so the head dips below hip level. As the head drops, the controller circles an arm under the chin and sprawls back, pinning the head against the chest with body weight driving down through the neck.
