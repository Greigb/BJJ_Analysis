# Electric chair

## Used from

- [[Half Guard (Bottom)]]

## How to Identify

The bottom player has isolated one of the top player's legs deep between their thighs, hugging it tight to their chest while their hips shift underneath, threading one leg across the front of the trapped knee and the other behind the ankle to torque the knee outward. The trapped leg is bent and pried open at an unnatural angle, with the bottom player's shoulders rotated and posted to drive the foot away from the centerline while the top player typically posts on hands trying to defend the twisting pressure on the knee.
