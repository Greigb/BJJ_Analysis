# Smash pass

## Used from

- [[Half Guard (Top)]]
- [[Headquarters / Passing Position]]
- [[Pass in Progress]]

## How to Identify

The top player drives chest-down pressure with a tight crossface or underhook, hips low and pressing forward to smash the bottom player's knees together or flat across their own body. One knee staples or splits the bottom legs while the other posts wide for base, and the bottom player's hips are pinned or rotated sideways with no space to frame or recover guard.
