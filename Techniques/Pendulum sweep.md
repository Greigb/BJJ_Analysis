# Pendulum sweep

## Used from

- [[Sweep in Progress]]

## How to Identify

Bottom player is on their back with one of the top player's sleeves or wrists gripped and the opposite-side ankle or pant cuff trapped; their legs swing in opposite directions like a pendulum, the free leg kicking high while hips rotate hard toward the gripped arm. The top player tips sideways over the trapped knee, unable to post because the wrist is pinned.
