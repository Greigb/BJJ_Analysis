# Berimbolo

## Used from

- [[De La Riva Guard]]

## How to Identify

Bottom player is inverted with shoulders on the mat and hips elevated, spinning beneath the top player while a De La Riva hook stays wrapped behind their lead leg. Far-side grip controls the ankle or belt as the rotation drives the standing player's hips off balance and exposes the back.
