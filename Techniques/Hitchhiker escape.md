# Hitchhiker escape

## Used from

- [[Submission Defence (escaping)]]

## How to Identify

The trapped arm rotates internally with the thumb pointed down toward the attacker's feet, breaking the straight-line extension of the armbar. The defender simultaneously turns belly-down, rolling the near shoulder over and spiraling toward the attacker's legs as the elbow corkscrews free of the hip. The attacker's legs remain across the chest while the defender's hips drive away from the fulcrum.
