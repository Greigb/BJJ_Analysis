# Far side armbar

## Used from

- [[Knee on Belly]]

## How to Identify

The attacker sits perpendicular to the opponent with one leg swung over the head to the far side, isolating the far arm between their thighs. The trapped arm is extended thumb-up with the elbow pinned against the attacker's hip or pelvis. Their seat stays low to the mat and knees squeeze tight as they arch back to hyperextend the joint.
