# Kimura

## Used from

- [[Closed Guard (Bottom)]]
- [[Side Control (Top)]]

## How to Identify

Look for a figure-four grip on one of the opponent's arms: one hand clamping their wrist while the other threads under their elbow to grip the attacker's own wrist. The trapped arm bends 90 degrees with the elbow pointing away from the torso, shoulder rotated and isolated as the attacker's chest weight pins the opponent flat.
