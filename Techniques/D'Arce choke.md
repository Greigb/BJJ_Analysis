# D'Arce choke

## Used from

- [[Front Headlock / Guillotine Position]]

## How to Identify

The attacker is positioned perpendicular to the defender, threading their arm deep across the back of the defender's neck and underneath the defender's near-side armpit, with the biceps clamping tight against the trapped shoulder. The attacker's hands are joined in a Gable or palm-to-palm grip locked against the far shoulder or their own biceps, while their chest drives down onto the back of the defender's head and trapped arm. The defender's near arm is pinned across their own throat, sandwiched between their neck and the attacker's squeezing biceps, often with the attacker's hips sprawled out low and pressuring forward.
