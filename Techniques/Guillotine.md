# Guillotine

## Used from

- [[Butterfly Guard]]
- [[Closed Guard (Bottom)]]

## How to Identify

The attacker's arm is wrapped around the front of the defender's neck with the defender's head trapped low and tucked against the attacker's ribcage or under the armpit, while the attacker's hands are clasped together (gable or palm-to-palm grip, or gripping own wrist) below the defender's chin. The choking forearm cuts across the throat with the blade of the wrist pulled upward and into the neck, and the defender's posture is broken sharply forward with their spine rounded. From butterfly, the attacker sits chest-to-chest with the head pinched between arm and torso; from closed guard, the attacker is on their back pulling the trapped head down toward their own sternum as the legs squeeze the waist.
