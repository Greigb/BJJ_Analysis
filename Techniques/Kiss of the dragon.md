# Kiss of the dragon

## Used from

- [[De La Riva Guard]]

## How to Identify

Bottom player is inverted with shoulders on the mat and hips rotating upward, corkscrewing under the standing opponent while the DLR hook stays engaged behind the lead leg. A far-ankle grip is typically maintained as the bottom player's head travels past the outside of the trailing leg, breaking the opponent's posture forward.
