# Can opener (illegal in some rulesets)

## Used from

- [[Inside Closed Guard (Top)]]
- [[Mount (Top)]]
- [[North-South (Top)]]

## How to Identify

The top player's hands are clasped behind the bottom player's head or cupping the back of the skull, actively curling the chin down toward the chest while the elbows pinch inward. Look for the bottom player's neck flexed forcefully, head lifted off the mat, and the top player's weight driving downward through the arms to crank the cervical spine.
