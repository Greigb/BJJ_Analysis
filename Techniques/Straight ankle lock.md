# Straight ankle lock

## Used from

- [[Single Leg X / Ashi Garami]]
- [[Straight Ashi Garami]]
- [[Ushiro Ashi Garami (Rear Ashi)]]

## How to Identify

Opponent's foot is trapped HIGH in the attacker's armpit with the blade of the forearm wedged UNDER the Achilles tendon. Hands clasped in a gable or figure-four grip against the chest while the attacker bridges the hips UPWARD and arches BACK, knees pinching the trapped leg to prevent rotational escape. Finishes primarily from `straight_ashi`, `single_leg_x`, or `outside_ashi`. **Disambiguator vs `heel_hook`:** NO rotational component — attacker extends the ankle joint (Achilles tendon stretch) without twisting the heel. Opponent's knee stays RELATIVELY STRAIGHT (no knee-line rotation). **vs `toe_hold`:** grip is under the Achilles, not on the toes/metatarsals.
