# Straight ankle lock

## Used from

- [[Single Leg X / Ashi Garami]]
- [[Straight Ashi Garami]]
- [[Ushiro Ashi Garami (Rear Ashi)]]

## How to Identify

Opponent's foot is trapped high in the attacker's armpit with the blade of the forearm wedged under the Achilles tendon. Hands are clasped in a gable or figure-four grip against the chest while the attacker bridges the hips upward and arches back, knees pinching the trapped leg to prevent rotational escape.
