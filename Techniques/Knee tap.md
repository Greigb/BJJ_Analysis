# Knee tap

## Used from

- [[Clinch / Collar Tie]]
- [[Unclear / Cannot Determine]]

## How to Identify

The attacker drives forward into the opponent with an underhook or collar tie while their lead hand reaches down and cups the outside of the opponent's near knee, palm pressing inward. The shoulder and chest post against the opponent's upper body as the cupping hand pushes the knee across the centerline, collapsing the base while their weight drives diagonally through the trapped leg.
