# Shrimp to half guard

## Used from

- [[Bottom Side Control]]

## How to Identify

Bottom player is turned onto their side rather than flat, hips visibly escaped away from the opponent with forearm frames wedged into the near hip and shoulder to hold space. The inside knee threads under the opponent's hip and shoots across to trap one of the opponent's legs between their knees, while the outside leg shrimps free. Body weight shifts onto the far shoulder as the near elbow pulls tight to prevent the opponent re-flattening the pin.
