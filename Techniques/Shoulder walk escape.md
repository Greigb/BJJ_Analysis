# Shoulder walk escape

## Used from

- [[Back Taken (Defending)]]

## How to Identify

The defender lies chest-up with the attacker chest-to-back behind them, but the shoulders are walking down the mat while the hips bridge and post to unload the hooks. The attacker appears to ride up the body as the hooks loosen or peel, and the defender's head drives toward the floor past the attacker's hip while the hands stay fighting at the neck.
