# Crossface

## Used from

- [[Half Guard (Top)]]

## How to Identify

Top player's forearm or bicep presses across the bottom player's jaw, forcing their head to turn away from the attacker. Shoulder drives into the face with chest low and weight committed forward, pinning the head to the mat. Often paired with a far-side underhook flattening the bottom player out as the top player works to free the trapped leg.
