# Clock choke

## Used from

- [[Knee on Belly]]
- [[Turtle Attack (Top)]]

## How to Identify

Attacker has a deep cross-collar grip with four fingers inside the far collar on a turtled opponent, while the free hand posts on the mat or grips the belt or far lapel. Chest and shoulder drive down onto the back of the neck as the attacker's feet walk in a circular arc around the head.
