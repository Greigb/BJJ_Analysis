# Snap down

## Used from

- [[Standing - Neutral]]

## How to Identify

Attacker clamps a collar tie or two hands on the back of the opponent's head and drives their weight sharply downward, breaking posture so the head drops below the hips. The opponent's hands shoot toward the mat to post as the attacker steps back to amplify the pull, shoulders stacked over the sinking head.
