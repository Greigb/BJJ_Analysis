# Go-behind

## Used from

- [[Front Headlock / Guillotine Position]]
- [[Wrestling Scramble]]

## How to Identify

Look for hands transitioning from the head/neck to the far armpit or shoulder while the attacker's hips rotate from a sprawl to a perpendicular angle alongside the opponent. The lead foot steps past the defender's hip and the chest turns toward their back, with the defender's posture broken and head still hanging down.
