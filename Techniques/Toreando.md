# Toreando

## Used from

- [[Pass in Progress]]

## How to Identify

The top player has both hands gripping the bottom player's pants at the knees or shins, arms extended to pin the legs down and across the mat while stepping wide to one side. Their hips are elevated and squared over the opponent's thighs, using the pant grips to steer the legs away as they circle past the knee line toward side control. Weight drives forward through the arms into the legs, keeping the opponent flat and unable to reinsert guard.
