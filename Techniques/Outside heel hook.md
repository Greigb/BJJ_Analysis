# Outside heel hook

## Used from

- [[Cross Ashi Garami]]
- [[Outside Ashi Garami]]
- [[Outside Sankaku (411)]]

## How to Identify

The opponent's foot is trapped at the attacker's chest with the heel exposed OUTWARD, a figure-four grip driving the forearm blade across the Achilles. The attacker's hips face away, often belly-down in outside sankaku/411, and the trapped knee rotates inward while the foot twists OUTWARD — the misalignment signaling active heel torque. Finishes from `outside_ashi`, `cross_ashi`, or outside sankaku (411). **Disambiguator vs `inside_heel_hook`:** the twist direction is OUTWARD (away from the opponent's midline); the exposed heel is on the OUTSIDE of the attacker. **vs `toe_hold`:** the grip crosses the Achilles/heel, not the toes.
