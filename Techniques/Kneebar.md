# Kneebar

## Used from

- [[50/50]]
- [[80/20 (Dominant 50/50)]]
- [[Cross Ashi Garami]]
- [[Outside Ashi Garami]]
- [[Outside Sankaku (411)]]

## How to Identify

The attacker's hips are clamped tight against the opponent's thigh with the isolated leg extended straight and the foot trapped high against the chest or shoulder. The attacker's pelvis sits past the opponent's kneecap and bridges upward to hyperextend the joint, knees pinched together to pin the thigh, with the captured heel often rotated skyward.
