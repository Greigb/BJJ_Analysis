# Kneebar

## Used from

- [[50/50]]
- [[80/20 (Dominant 50/50)]]
- [[Cross Ashi Garami]]
- [[Outside Ashi Garami]]
- [[Outside Sankaku (411)]]

## How to Identify

Attacker's hips are clamped tight against the opponent's thigh with the isolated leg extended STRAIGHT and the foot trapped high against the chest or shoulder. Attacker's pelvis sits PAST the opponent's kneecap and bridges upward to hyperextend the joint, knees pinched together to pin the thigh, with the captured heel often rotated skyward. Finishes from `straight_ashi`, `outside_ashi`, 50/50 (with leg extension), or honey-hole extension. **Disambiguator vs `heel_hook`:** opponent's leg is EXTENDED STRAIGHT, not bent — no knee-line rotation off-center. **vs `calf_slicer`:** attacker's hip at the knee acts as a fulcrum for HYPEREXTENSION (not a shin wedged behind the knee for compression). The finishing signal is hip extension, not leg flexion.
