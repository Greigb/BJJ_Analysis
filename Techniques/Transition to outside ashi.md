# Transition to outside ashi

## Used from

- [[Straight Ashi Garami]]

## How to Identify

The attacker's top leg lifts and swings across the opponent's trapped thigh while the bottom leg stays underneath, so the two legs close on opposite sides of the captured limb. The hips rotate away from the opponent and the body angle shifts more perpendicular, with ankle or foot control usually retained throughout the switch.
