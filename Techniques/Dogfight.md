# Dogfight

## Used from

- [[Half Guard (Bottom)]]

## How to Identify

Both players are upright on their knees chest-to-chest, with the bottom player having come up to the knees from half guard; the attacker's head is glued to the opponent's near shoulder while one arm is deeply underhooked and the other wraps the far side, often gripping his own wrist or the opponent's back. Hips are squared and driving forward into the opponent, with the trapped leg still threaded between the opponent's legs, creating the characteristic head-to-head, shoulder-pressure stalemate before the finish.
