# Sweep

## Used from

- [[Dogfight / Underhook Battle]]
- [[Single Leg X / Ashi Garami]]
- [[Ushiro Ashi Garami (Rear Ashi)]]

## How to Identify

Mid-sweep, the bottom player drives hips up or laterally as the opponent's base collapses over one shoulder, weight tipping past their post. Underhooks or leg frames elevate one side while grips pull the other down, catching the pair mid-rotation rather than settled on top or bottom.
