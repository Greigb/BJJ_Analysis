# Seated guard pull

## Used from

- [[Guard Recovery (in progress)]]

## How to Identify

Bottom player is upright on their tailbone with hips just off the mat, one hand posted behind for base and the other reaching for a collar or sleeve grip. At least one leg threads between the bodies — typically a shin across the thigh or a foot on the hip — while the opposite knee stays pulled in tight. Head is up, chest squared to the opponent, and the prior shrimping motion has settled into a seated stance.
