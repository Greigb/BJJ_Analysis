# Triangle

## Used from

- [[Closed Guard (Bottom)]]

## How to Identify

The attacker's legs form a figure-four around the top person's neck and one shoulder — one thigh across the neck, the opposite shin locked over that knee. One of the opponent's arms is trapped inside the legs while the other remains outside, their head pulled down and posture broken as the attacker angles off to the side.
