# Stack defence

## Used from

- [[Submission Defence (escaping)]]

## How to Identify

The defender postures up and drives weight forward onto the attacker's hips, stacking their shoulders into the mat with legs folded overhead. The threatened arm is clamped to the chest, free hand gripping the trapped wrist to block extension, while the head presses into the attacker's thigh and feet walk toward the head-side.
