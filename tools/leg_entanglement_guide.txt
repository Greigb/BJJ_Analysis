LEG ENTANGLEMENT DECISION TREE
================================
When you identify the category as "leg_entanglement" (both players' legs are
intertwined/tangled, legs are the central focus), use this decision tree to
identify the SPECIFIC position:

QUESTION 1: Are both players' legs MIRRORED (symmetrical wrapping)?
  Each person has one leg wrapped around the other's leg in a similar way.

  YES (symmetrical/mutual wrapping) →
    QUESTION 2: Are both players facing the SAME direction?
      YES → QUESTION 3: Does one player have their outside leg crossing OVER the opponent's hip?
        YES → "eighty_twenty" (80/20 — the player with the leg over has dominant control)
        NO  → "fifty_fifty" (50/50 — symmetrical, neither has advantage)
      NO (one player facing away) → "backside_fifty_fifty"

  NO (asymmetric — one player has dominant leg control) →
    QUESTION 2: Are the attacker's legs TRIANGLED (crossed/figure-four locked) around ONE of the opponent's legs?

      YES (triangle/figure-four grip around one leg) →
        QUESTION 3: Is the attacker facing TOWARD the opponent?
          YES → "inside_sankaku" (Honeyhole/Saddle — strongest heel hook position.
                 The trapped leg goes BETWEEN the attacker's thighs.)
          NO (attacker facing AWAY / belly-down) → "outside_sankaku" (411 —
                 used for outside heel hooks. Triangle is on the outside of the thigh.)

      NO (no triangle grip) →
        QUESTION 3: Is the attacker on their BACK with one foot on the opponent's HIP?
          YES → "single_leg_x" (SLX/Ashi Garami — one foot on hip, one hooking
                 behind knee. Opponent usually standing or kneeling.)
          NO →
            QUESTION 4: Does the attacker's OUTSIDE leg cross OVER the opponent's body/hip?
              YES → "outside_ashi" (Outside Ashi — more control than straight ashi)
              NO →
                QUESTION 5: Is the attacker BEHIND the opponent's leg (facing the back of the knee)?
                  YES → "ushiro_ashi" (Rear Ashi — attacking from behind the leg)
                  NO →
                    QUESTION 6: Are the attacker's legs crossing to the OPPOSITE side of the opponent's body?
                      YES → "cross_ashi" (Cross Ashi — transitional position)
                      NO  → "straight_ashi" (Straight Ashi — basic position,
                             both legs on same side, hips beside the knee)

COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID:
- 50/50 vs inside_sankaku: 50/50 is SYMMETRICAL (mirrored). Inside sankaku is ASYMMETRIC (one person has triangle grip).
- 80/20 vs 50/50: Look for the outside leg crossing over the hip. If one player has it, it's 80/20. If neither does, it's 50/50.
- inside_sankaku vs outside_sankaku: Inside = attacker faces TOWARD opponent. Outside = attacker faces AWAY.
- straight_ashi vs outside_ashi: Outside ashi has the outside leg crossing OVER the hip. Straight ashi does NOT.
- single_leg_x vs straight_ashi: SLX has a foot on the hip (elevated position). Straight ashi is flat with hips beside the knee.
