## Coach's Notes

Three clear patterns emerge from this analysis with the decision tree:

1. **Anthony's 50/50 game:** He pulled you in three times. The decision tree correctly identifies these as fifty_fifty (mirrored, same direction, no outside leg dominance). The critical moment is when Q1 switches from NO to YES — that's when the legs become mirrored and the 50/50 locks. **Backstep at the first sign of mirrored wrapping.**

2. **The reguard cycle:** You passed 4+ times but Anthony always recovered within ~10 seconds. Side control is not a destination — it's a waypoint. **Pass → mount within 3 seconds.**

3. **The closed guard sweep:** Same vulnerability as every previous analysis. Broken posture on the knees = sweep. **Stand up to break closed guard. Every time.**
