## Summary

After the tap in Roll 1, both players reset and continue. Anthony sweeps Greig from closed guard at 2:40 and spends the remainder on top, advancing through side control → north-south → mount. Greig shows resilience recovering to open guard at the end.

## Top 3 Improvements

1. **Closed guard posture** — Got swept from broken posture. STAND UP to break guard.
2. **Bottom recovery speed** — After sweep, gave up positions too quickly. Frame and shrimp immediately.
3. **Active bottom guard** — Need frames and foot placement when on bottom, not passive defence.

## Coach's Notes

The bottom game needs specific work. The closed guard sweep is a recurring vulnerability — broken posture on the knees leads to the sweep every time. After being swept, the positional loss was rapid: side control → north-south → mount in about 45 seconds. Practice: (1) standing guard breaks from inside closed guard, (2) frame-and-shrimp from bottom side control, (3) trap-and-roll from bottom mount.
