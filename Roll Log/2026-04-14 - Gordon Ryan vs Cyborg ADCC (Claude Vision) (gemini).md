## Top Improvements

1. Cyborg needs to address the knee line immediately when Gordon enters the legs — once inside sankaku is locked, escape is extremely difficult
2. Standing phase: use more proactive attacks rather than purely reactive clinch fighting against a guard-pulling opponent
3. Guard passing: when on top briefly, needed more urgency to pass before Gordon could re-establish guard and transition to leg attacks

## Strengths Observed

- Gordon: Elite composure and patience — waited for the right moment to enter the legs rather than forcing it
- Gordon: Seamless transition chain from seated guard → SLX → inside sankaku showing mastery of the Danaher leg lock system

Once on the ground, the leg entry sequence was clinical.
